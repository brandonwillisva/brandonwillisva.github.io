## 📂 Projects

### 📈 Investment Dashboard
**File:** `stock-dashboard.html`
**Live:** [brandonwillisva.github.io/stock-dashboard.html](https://brandonwillisva.github.io/stock-dashboard.html)

This was my first real coding project. I built a stock tracking dashboard that lets you look at price charts and key stats for 5 companies I actually care about:

- **AAPL** — Apple
- **NVDA** — Nvidia (they make the chips that power AI)
- **SPY** — S&P 500 ETF (tracks the top 500 companies in the U.S.)
- **BAH** — Booz Allen Hamilton (they do AI and tech consulting for the government, based near me in Northern Virginia)
- **AMZN** — Amazon (I visited their HQ in Arlington through the Amazon AHEAD program)

You can switch between stocks and change the time range (1 week, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months).

**Built with:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Chart.js

**What I want to add next:** Connect it to a real stock API so the prices update live, and add a portfolio tracker where you can see how much your investments are worth.
